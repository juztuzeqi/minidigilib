📖 Text 1: Rina
Rina is a student, she is ten years old. She lives in a small house with her parents. Every morning, Rina wakes up at six o'clock. She brushes her teeth and takes a shower. Then she has breakfast with her family.

Rina has a blue bag. In her bag, there are books, a blue pencil and pink eraser. She goes to school at seven o'clock. She walks to school with her best friend, Siti. They are always happy.
🏠 Text 2: Tony and His Family
Today is Sunday. Tony and his family are at home. Tony is sitting at the table. He is eating bread and drinking milk. His mother is in the kitchen. She is cooking fried chicken, his favorite food. His father is in the living room. He is watching the news on TV. Tony's brother is on the floor. He is playing with his toys, he is happy.
