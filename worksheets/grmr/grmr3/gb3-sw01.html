Unit 1: Identifying Nouns in Sentences
What is a Noun?

Noun: A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

Nouns are the "naming words" in our sentences. They tell us WHO or WHAT we are talking about.

Examples:

  • Person: teacher, student, doctor, Ali, Sarah
  • Place: school, park, hospital, Jakarta, library
  • Thing: book, pencil, table, computer, apple
  • Idea: happiness, love, time, friendship

How to find nouns in a sentence:

1. Ask "Who?" or "What?" about the verb.
2. Look for words that can have "a", "an", or "the" before them.
3. Nouns can be singular (one) or plural (more than one).
