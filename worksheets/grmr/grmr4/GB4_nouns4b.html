Quick Rule Summary:

  • Some: Used in positive sentences and questions when offering or requesting.
  • Any: Used in negative sentences and most questions.
  • Example: "I have some apples." (positive)
  • Example: "I don't have any apples." (negative)
  • Example: "Do you have any apples?" (question)
  • Example: "Would you like some apples?" (offering)
